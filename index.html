Finally, HITCON will present you "Attack & Defense"!

We are pleased to announce that the HITCON CTF "Attack & Defense" competition is about to start.

Time for qualification is Nov 4 10:00 AM ~ Nov 6 10:00 PM (GMT+8, 48 hours)

Top 10 international teams and top 2 Taiwan teams will be invited to HITCON CTF Final on Dec 8-9
( Onsite Attack & Defense in Taipei, 4 players / team )

Please keep following our official website to get more details:
http://ctf.hitcon.org

競賽說明

  • 10/28 00:00 開放線上註冊
  • 預賽為 48 小時的線上 Jeopardy 形式
  • 競賽採取積分累計制，比賽結束後依分數高低判定優勝，提供決賽資格予優勝者。同分者，依最後一次正確提交的時間判定
  • 比賽前十名的國際隊伍以及前兩名的台灣隊伍，將晉級 12 月 8 - 9 號於台北舉行的 HITCON CTF Final (現場 Attack & Defense, 每隊 4 人)
  • 主辦單位將 "部分" 補助前十名隊伍參加決賽所需之交通及住宿費用
  • 2017 HITCON CTF 資格賽：
    下列競賽之冠軍隊伍將自動晉級決賽，不需參與 11/4 舉辦之預賽
    • TrendMicro CTF 2017
    • AIS3 CTF 2017
  • Flag 形式為: hitcon{printable ascii+}
  • 出題關主與工作人員均不列入成績計算

Contest Information

  • The registration link is available from 10/28.
  • Qualification is online jeopardy for 48 hours.
  • Solving each problem will get certain points. Winners will be ranked by points.If the points are the same, the winners will be ranked by time of the last correct submission.
  • Top 10 international teams and top 2 Taiwan teams will be invited to HITCON CTF Final on Dec 8-9 (Onsite Attack & Defense in Taipei, 4 players / team)
  • We will "partially" support top 10 teams for their flight and accommodation.
  • Currently 2017 HITCON CTF Qualifying Contests:
    The champions of the following contests will pre-qualify directly for the final in December and do not need to participate in the preliminary in 4th November.
    • TrendMicro CTF 2017
    • AIS3 CTF 2017
  • Flag format: hitcon{printable ascii+}
  • Game makers and staffs will not be counted in the ranking process.

  • 決賽: 12/8(五) ~ 12/9(六)

  • 臺灣隊伍特殊獎: $1,000 USD

Awards in final

  • 1st place: $10,000 USD
  • 2nd place: $5,000 USD
  • 3rd place: $2,000 USD
  • Special award for Top 1 Taiwan team: $1,000 USD

比賽注意事項

競賽期間，做出以下違規事項者將直接取消資格：

  • 進行任何破壞競賽公平公正原則之行為
  • 散播、與其它隊伍交換關卡金鑰
  • 除競賽中提供的關卡外，禁止對競賽系統、參賽者及其它人員進行攻擊

每位參賽者最多僅能參與一個隊伍

Contest rules

During the contest, any of the following behaviors will lead to disqualification:

  • Any behavior that compromises the fairness of contest
  • Publishing, exchanging problem solutions
  • Attacking any personnel, including participants and non-participants, or the contest system itself

Each player can only join one team
